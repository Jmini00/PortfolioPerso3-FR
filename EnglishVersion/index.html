About Me

Full Stack Developer!

Passionate about web development, I dedicate my free time to perfecting and enhancing my skills. I am currently seeking an opportunity within a company that will allow me to deepen and broaden my expertise.
My goal is to gain valuable professional experience that will help me grow and contribute meaningfully in the field of web development.
Curious and eager to learn, I continuously train, practice, and expand my knowledge daily through online courses and the completion of various personal projects. I am looking for a new challenge and am motivated to apply my knowledge and learn from working with a team!

Read More

Graduated with the RNCP 37273 Full-Stack Web Developer certification, I have a strong interest and genuine curiosity for learning in this field, which I find truly fascinating. This passion led me to dive into development as a self-taught individual before starting my formal training, and I continue to enhance my knowledge and skills through online courses (OpenClassrooms / Udemy).
I began a Web Developer/Web Mobile training program at WebForce3 in October 2023 and completed nearly the entire curriculum. However, the closure of the institution two weeks before the end of the program in January 2024 prompted me to join 3WAcademy to complete my training, obtain my certification, and earn my diploma.
